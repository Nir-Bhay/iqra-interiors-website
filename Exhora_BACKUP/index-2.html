Questions & answers

Have Any Questions? Find Answers Here!

  • What types of exhibition stalls do you design?
    We design and fabricate a wide range of exhibition stalls including custom-built booths, modular stalls, portable displays, double-decker structures, and island layouts for trade shows and exhibitions of all sizes.
  • How long does the stall design process take?
    Our standard turnaround is 2-3 weeks from concept approval to final installation. For urgent requirements, we offer expedited timelines. We recommend reaching out at least 4-6 weeks before your event for the best results.
  • What materials do you use for fabrication?
    We use premium-grade materials including laminated MDF, acrylic, aluminum profiles, fabric graphics, LED lighting systems, and eco-friendly options. All materials are chosen for durability, visual impact, and easy transport.
  • Do you handle on-site installation and dismantling?
    Yes, we provide complete turnkey services. Our dedicated crew handles transportation, on-site assembly, electrical connections, and post-event dismantling. You just show up and focus on your business.
  • What is the typical cost of an exhibition stall?
    Costs vary based on stall size, design complexity, materials, and exhibition venue requirements. We offer competitive pricing with transparent quotes. Contact us with your specifications for a free consultation and detailed estimate.
