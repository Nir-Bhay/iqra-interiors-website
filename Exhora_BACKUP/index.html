We design a full range of exhibition stalls - from compact pavilion booths and shell scheme setups to large custom-built, double-storey stands. Our portfolio includes product launch booths, conference pavilions, and multi-brand exhibition environments for trade shows, expos, and corporate events across India.

Our typical timeline is 10-21 working days from brief to finished 3D design approval, depending on complexity. For fabrication and on-site installation, we factor in 3-5 additional days. We also handle urgent requirements - contact us if you have a tight deadline and we'll work out a fast-track schedule.

We use premium-grade materials including MS/aluminium framing, PVC foam boards, acrylic, fabric tension systems, LED lighting, laminated sunboards, and high-quality vinyl graphics. All materials are sourced for durability, visual impact, and ease of transport - ensuring every stall looks outstanding and ships safely.

Yes, absolutely. Our team manages end-to-end execution - fabrication at our workshop, logistics to the venue, on-site installation before the event, supervision throughout, and complete dismantling and storage after it ends. You focus on your business; we handle everything on the ground.

Costs vary based on stall size, design complexity, materials, and exhibition venue requirements. We offer competitive pricing with transparent quotes. Contact us with your specifications for a free consultation and detailed estimate - no hidden charges, no surprises.

We work across India - Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Noida, and beyond. Our team has successfully executed projects at major exhibition centres including Pragati Maidan (Delhi), Bombay Exhibition Centre (Mumbai), and BIEC (Bengaluru). Just tell us your venue and we'll coordinate everything remotely.
