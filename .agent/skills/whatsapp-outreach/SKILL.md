# WhatsApp Outreach Skill

> **Purpose:** Master WhatsApp Business as a channel for personalized, high-conversion outreach to local business owners

---

## 🎯 When to Use

Use this skill when:
- Reaching out to local business owners in India
- Need quick, direct communication
- Want high open rates (98% for WhatsApp)
- Building relationships with SMB owners

---

## ⚠️ Golden Rules

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **WhatsApp Outreach Rules:**
> 1. NEVER send more than 30 new messages per day
> 2. NEVER message before 9 AM or after 9 PM
> 3. NEVER send media in first message
> 4. ALWAYS personalize (name, business, observation)
> 5. ALWAYS wait 24 hours before following up
> 6. Use WhatsApp Business account (not personal)

---

## 📱 Message Structure

### Anatomy of a Great WhatsApp Message
```
[Greeting + Name] ← Personalization
[Observation about their business] ← Shows research
[Quick value proposition] ← What's in it for them
[Simple question/CTA] ← Easy to respond
[Your name + business] ← Credibility
```

**Length Guidelines:**
- First message: 3-5 sentences
- Follow-up: 2-3 sentences
- Maximum: 500 characters

## 📝 Opening Message Templates

### Template 1: Observation Hook
```
Hi [Name] ji 🙏

I was looking at [Business Name] on Google - [specific observation like "4.5 stars with 200+ reviews - impressive!"]

Quick question: Are you getting customers from online searches, or mostly walk-ins?

We help [niche] businesses in [area] get found on Google. Happy to share how.

- Nirbhay, WebSkale
```

### Template 2: Problem Hook
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [Business Name] doesn't show up when I search "[niche] in [area]" on Google.

This means potential customers searching for [what they offer] can't find you.

Would you be interested in seeing how your competitors are ranking?

- Nirbhay
```

### Template 3: Competitor Reference
```
Hi [Name] ji,

I recently helped [similar business/area] with their website. They're now getting [X] inquiries from Google.

I noticed [Business Name] could benefit from something similar.

Worth a quick chat?

- Nirbhay, WebSkale
```

### Template 4: Value-First
```
Hi [Name],

I put together 3 quick ideas for how [Business Name] could get more customers online. No sales pitch - just ideas.

Would you like me to share them?

- Nirbhay
```

### Template 5: Referral Mention
```
Hi [Name] ji,

[Referrer Name] suggested I reach out to you.

We helped him with his website and he mentioned you might be looking for something similar.

Do you have 5 minutes to chat this week?

- Nirbhay
```

## 💬 Response Handling Scripts

### When They Ask About Price
```
Great question!

It depends on what you need:
📱 Basic website: ₹15,000-25,000
🛒 With ordering/booking: ₹25,000-45,000
⚡ Full custom: ₹50,000+

What features would be most useful for [Business Name]?
```

### When They Say "Send Details"
```
Sure! Here's a quick overview:

📋 What we do:
- Custom websites for [niche]
- Mobile-friendly design
- Google optimization
- WhatsApp integration

💰 Starting at ₹[X]
⏰ Ready in 2-3 weeks

🔗 Our work: webskale.tech

Can I call you for 5 minutes to understand your needs better?
```

### When They Say "Not Interested"
```
No problem at all! 🙏

If you ever change your mind, feel free to reach out.

Wishing you all the best with [Business Name]!
```

### When They Say "Already Have Website"
```
That's great! Can I take a quick look?

[After checking]
I noticed [specific issue like mobile, speed, design].

Would you be interested in a free audit? I can share 3 quick improvements.

No obligation - just helpful insights!
```

### When They Say "Too Expensive"
```
I understand budget is important.

A few options:
1. Start with a basic site now, upgrade later
2. Payment in 2-3 installments
3. Remove [feature] to reduce cost

What works best for your situation?
```

### When They Say "I'll Think About It"
```
Of course! Take your time.

One thing to consider: Every day without a website, people searching for "[niche] in [area]" are finding your competitors instead.

Can I check in next week?
```

### When They Say "Yes, Let's Talk"
```
Great! 🎉

When works best for a quick call?
- Today [time]?
- Tomorrow [time]?

Or tell me your preferred time and I'll call.

Looking forward to it!
```

## 🚫 What NOT to Do

> [!CAUTION]
> **Never:**
> - Copy-paste exact same message to everyone
> - Send voice notes as first contact
> - Send images/videos in first message
> - Message the same person twice in one day
> - Use WhatsApp broadcast (looks spammy)
> - Add people to groups without permission
> - Message on Sundays or late nights
> - Use excessive emojis (1-2 max)
