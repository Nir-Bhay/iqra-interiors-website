# Market Intelligence Skill

> **Purpose:** Provide frameworks for researching industries, analyzing competitors, and sizing markets

---

## 🎯 When to Use

Use this skill when:
- Researching a new niche to target
- Analyzing competitor pricing and positioning
- Understanding market size and opportunity
- Preparing for lead generation campaigns

---

## 📊 Industry Research Framework

### Step 1: Market Overview
```markdown
## [Industry] Market Research

### Market Size
- Total businesses in India: [number]
- Total in target cities: [number]
- Annual growth rate: [X]%

### Industry Trends
- [Trend 1 affecting digital presence]
- [Trend 2]
- [Trend 3]

### Digital Maturity
- % with websites: [estimate]
- % with modern websites: [estimate]
- % on social media: [estimate]
- Common platforms: [list]
```

### Step 2: Target Segment Analysis
```markdown
## Target Segment: [Niche] in [City]

### Segment Size
- Estimated businesses: [number]
- Premium tier (high budget): [X]%
- Mid tier (target): [X]%
- Budget tier: [X]%

### Buying Behavior
- Average website budget: ₹[X]-[Y]
- Decision timeline: [X] weeks
- Key decision makers: [roles]
- Purchase triggers: [events]

### Competition for Their Attention
- Other agencies targeting them: [list]
- DIY alternatives they use: [Wix, etc.]
- Price sensitivity: [Low/Medium/High]
```

## 🔍 Competitor Analysis Template

### Direct Competitors (Web Agencies)
```markdown
## Competitor: [Agency Name]

### Overview
- Website: [URL]
- Location: [City]
- Size: [Team size estimate]
- Years in business: [X]

### Positioning
- Tagline: [Their tagline]
- Primary message: [What they lead with]
- Target audience: [Who they serve]

### Services & Pricing
| Service | Their Price | Our Price | Advantage |
|---------|------------|-----------|-----------|
| [Service 1] | ₹[X] | ₹[X] | [Who's better] |
| [Service 2] | ₹[X] | ₹[X] | [Who's better] |

### Portfolio Analysis
- Quality level: [1-10]
- Industries served: [list]
- Showcase projects: [notable ones]

### Strengths
- [Strength 1]
- [Strength 2]

### Weaknesses (Our Opportunity)
- [Weakness 1]
- [Weakness 2]

### How to Beat Them
- [Strategy 1]
- [Strategy 2]
```

### Indirect Competitors (DIY, Templates)
```markdown
## Alternative: [Platform Name, e.g., Wix]

### What They Offer
- Price: ₹[X]/month
- Features: [list key features]
- Ease of use: [1-10]

### Why Clients Choose Them
- [Reason 1]
- [Reason 2]

### Why Clients Should Choose Us Instead
- [Counterpoint 1]
- [Counterpoint 2]

### Talking Points Against
- "[Script for when client mentions this option]"
```

## 🔬 Research Sources

|--------|-----------------|----------|
| Google Maps | Business listings, ratings, websites | High |

## 📋 Weekly Research Routine

### Monday: Niche Focus
- Pick 1 niche to research deeply
- Complete industry overview
- Identify pain points

### Tuesday: City Analysis
- Analyze 1 target city
- Count businesses in niche
- Assess competition

### Wednesday: Competitor Watch
- Check 2-3 competitor agencies
- Note new projects/clients
- Update competitive intelligence

### Thursday: Trend Spotting
- Read industry news
- Check social media trends
- Note content opportunities

### Friday: Synthesis
- Update research database
- Prepare insights for team
- Plan next week's focus
