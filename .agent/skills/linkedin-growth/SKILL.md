# LinkedIn Growth Skill (2026 Edition)

Expert guide for building **Technical Authority** and **Meaningful Engagement**.
*Updated for the 2026 Algorithm Shift: Quality > Virality.*

## 🧠 The 2026 Algorithm Signals

1.  **Expertise & Relevance**: Content matching your profile's keywords (e.g., "React", "System Design") gets a boost. Stay in your lane.
2.  **Meaningful Comments**: "Great post!" is ignored. Queries, debates, and long-form comments drive distribution.
3.  **Dwell Time**: PDF Carousels and dense code snippets that make people stop and read are prioritized.
4.  **No External Links**: Links in the post body kill reach by ~60%. Put them in the comments or bio.

---

## 🏗️ The "Developer Authority" Strategy

Don't just be an "influencer". Be an **Engineer who shares**.

**1. The "Build in Public" Loop**
*   **Share the Struggle**: "Spent 4 hours debugging a CORS error." (Relatable)
*   **Share the Solution**: "Here is the one config line that fixed it." (Valuable)
*   **Share the Result**: "API latency dropped by 50%." (Impressive)

**2. Visual Technical Content**
*   **Annotated Code**: Screenshots of code with VS Code extensions (Carbon, Snappify). Use arrows to explain logic.
*   **Architecture Diagrams**: Simple boxes-and-arrows showing how your app works. (Mermaid, Excalidraw).
*   **Video Demos**: 30-90s Loom videos walking through a feature. Native video reach is high.

---

## ✍️ Writing for Engineers (The "No-Fluff" Rule)

*   **Hook**: State the Problem or the result immediately.
*   **Body**: Bullet points, code blocks, or data. No long paragraphs.
*   **Tone**: Professional, humble, yet authoritative. Avoid "Marketing Speak".

---

## 🚀 Engagement Protocol 2.0

1.  **The "Conversation Starter"**: End every post with a specific technical question.
    *   *Bad*: "Thoughts?"
    *   *Good*: "Do you prefer Zod or Joi for validation? Why?"
2.  **The "Community Service" Comment**: Find a post by a junior dev and answer their question thoroughly.
3.  **Reply Hygiene**: Reply to comments with a question to double the comment count (Conversation Loops).

---

## 📅 The 3-2-1 Posting Schedule

*   **3x High-Value**: Code tips, System Design breakdowns, or Project updates.
*   **2x Personal/Career**: Soft skills, interview tips, or "Day in the Life".
*   **1x Opinion/Hot Take**: "Why I stopped using Redux." (Sparks debate).
